Phase 3: Content Repurposing & Adaptation

AI Prompt: Blog Post to Twitter Thread

Convert this blog post into an engaging Twitter thread:

Original Content: [paste content]

Thread Requirements:
- 8-12 tweets maximum
- Tweet 1: Hook + promise of value
- Tweets 2-10: Key insights (one main point per tweet)
- Final tweet: Summary + CTA
- Use emojis and formatting for readability
- Each tweet under 280 characters
