Universal Authority Framework

  • 1. Experience-First: Frame content with personal or professional experience.
  • 2. Data-Driven: Include 1-2 relevant stats or credible sources.
  • 3. Practical Focus: Provide actionable steps, templates, or examples.
  • 4. Community-Centered: Ask questions and build on existing conversations.

30-Second Topic Analysis

Quickly assess your authority level to choose the right content angle.

Expert (8-10/10): Share advanced, contrarian insights.
Experienced (6-7/10): Document journeys, compare tools.
Learning (4-5/10): Chronicle exploration, share resources.
Curious (1-3/10): Interview experts, present research.

Universal Content Templates

The "Learning Journey"

Share what you learned about a topic after a specific experience. Structure: Context -> Discovery -> Insights -> Implementation.

The "Trend Analysis"

Offer a contrarian or deeper view on a popular topic. Structure: Current Narrative -> Deeper Data -> Contrarian View -> Implications.

The "Synthesis"

Connect two seemingly unrelated topics to reveal novel insights. Structure: Obvious Connection -> Deeper Integration -> Cross-Domain Learning.
